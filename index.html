Vivace Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

Vivace is a music-theory learning app. This policy explains what information the app collects, why, who we share it with, and the choices you have. We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the app and track your learning progress. We do not run ads, we do not track you across other apps or websites, and we do not sell your data.

Information we collect

Account & sign-in. When you create an account we store:

  • Your email address (with Sign in with Apple, you may choose to hide it, and the app still works without it).
  • Your name, if you choose to share it during Sign in with Apple.
  • An account identifier provided by Apple or Google to sign you in.
  • An optional username and profile picture, if you set them.

Your learning progress. To teach you and track your streaks, we store:

  • Lessons completed, scores, experience points (XP), level, and streak days.
  • Practice and quiz history, including questions answered and the answers you gave (so we can review your mistakes with you).
  • Achievements and badges you've earned.
  • Your in-app settings (sound, notation, haptics, notifications, theme, daily goal).

Subscriptions & purchases. Premium subscriptions are handled by Apple's App Store and by RevenueCat, our subscription provider. We receive your purchase history and current subscription status so we can unlock premium features. Payment card details are handled entirely by Apple, so we never see or store them.

Microphone (during singing exercises only). Some exercises listen to your singing to detect pitch in real time. This audio is analyzed on the spot and is never recorded, saved, or sent anywhere. The microphone is used only while you are doing such an exercise.

Camera & photos. Only used if you choose to set a profile picture. The image you pick is uploaded to store your avatar; we don't access your photo library otherwise.

Notifications. If you turn on streak or achievement reminders, the app sends you local notifications. You can turn these off at any time in the app or in your device settings.

What we do not collect

  • No location data.
  • No contacts, calendar, or health data.
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA) and no ad networks.
  • No third-party analytics or crash-reporting (e.g. no Google Analytics, Sentry, Facebook SDK).
  • No cross-app or cross-site tracking.

Who we share data with

We share data only with the service providers that make the app work, and only for that purpose:

  • Supabase: secure storage of your account and learning progress.
  • RevenueCat and Apple: processing subscriptions and purchases.
  • Apple and Google: verifying your identity when you sign in.

These providers are bound to protect your data and may only use it to provide their service to us. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

How long we keep your data

We keep your account and progress for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, we delete your associated personal data from our systems.

Your choices and rights

  • Access or correct your information from within the app's profile settings.
  • Delete your account and data: you can request deletion at any time by emailing us at the address below, and we will remove your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent: you can sign out, turn off notifications, or stop using the app at any time.

Children

Vivace is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions, or a request to delete your data? Email us at vivace.support@gmail.com.
